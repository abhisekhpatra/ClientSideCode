For each of chart in this section we ask you to choose colors that you think would be appropriate to convey the following impressions: Training 1(left) Happy and Training 2(right): Sad

Follow The following Steps:

1. Click on the color buttons Underneath "Training 1" and "Training 2". Once the color palette opens , you can choose a color by just selecting it from the palette.

2. For the Bar Chart (Training 1 and 2) you need to select 5 colors. It represents a categorical dataset, where each color refers to one category.

3. For each chart you can vary transparency to your choice by using the slider below the "Vary Transparency".

4. When you are finished with a chart, rate your satisfaction with how well you think the palette allowed you to communicate the overall impression using the scale below (1-being least satisfied to 10-being most satisfied). Once you have rated both charts in this training task, you finish your training and move to the main experiment.

5. Once both the tasks are rated it enables the button "Move to next Task". Click it and move to main experiment.
